Apps Files

Home Screen:

Shows a list of all the files in your application. Select one to edit that file.

Add Page:

The file image in the navbar allows you to add a page to your aplication.

When adding a page you will be asked to put the filename for the page you are adding and to choose a template.

Depending on file template you choose, you may be asked to add or omit the file extention when adding the file name. Be carefull to do so.

Export:

Allows you to make your app a stand alone desktop application on Linux, Mac, and Windows.

Once you click the export button. You will be greated with an alert asking you to unzip a zip containing the bace files for your application.

To finish the process click the export button again. At this point your stand alone applications have been created.

Logo:

Opens up the folder on your hard drive your app lives in.

Known Issues:

The list will not open folders in your app. To do that click on the logo to open up your apps folder and then you can click through its folders and open them up in your prefered IDE.

Note: For more information please visit: https://github.com/WebFra-me/WebDesktop/wiki
